🎯 Project Overview

BreakNWipe is a robust, secure, and standards-compliant data wiping solution designed to address India's e-waste crisis by providing trustworthy IT asset recycling through secure data sanitization.

India generates over 1.75 million tonnes of e-waste annually. A key barrier to responsible recycling is fear of data breaches, leading to over ₹50,000 crore worth of IT assets being hoarded instead of properly recycled.
