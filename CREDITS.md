## Project Vision

The **CodeBreakers Team** developed BreakNWipe to solve a critical problem in India's digital economy - the lack of trust in IT asset recycling due to data security concerns. Our solution provides:

- **Trustworthy Data Sanitization**: Standards-compliant wiping with verifiable certificates
- **Accessible Technology**: User-friendly interface suitable for general public use
- **Transparent Process**: QR codes and digital signatures for third-party verification
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: Support for all major storage technologies and standards
